About me

Science, care, & curiosity.

I'm Nicolas Roguski, a Biomedical Physiology student at Simon Fraser University, driven by a fascination with how the human body works at every scale — from cellular signalling to whole-system function. I'm passionate about bridging foundational science with real-world health applications.

My studies at SFU have immersed me in physiology, anatomy, biochemistry, and neuroscience, building a rigorous scientific foundation that fuels my curiosity and my ambition to pursue graduate-level research or a career in the health sciences.

Outside the lab and lecture hall, I'm always exploring new ideas — whether that's a journal deep-dive, a new research thread, or a conversation that challenges my thinking.
